# Reallocate 77.5% of Weekly Emissions to the SupplyWatcher (Burn) starting from Epoch 227

As documented and prepared in 2024, the Qubic emission/supply schedule foresees a halving in EP227.

## Proposal
Allow adjustment of the Computor Donation for the SupplyWatcher (burn).

---

## Voting Options

- **Option 0:** No, I don't want
- **Option 1:** Yes, adjust computor donation in EP227 (Approve Halving at Epoch 227 that reduces half of effective emissions)

---

## 	Summary

This proposal recommends cutting Qubic’s weekly emissions in half at Epoch 227 to keep us on track with the long-term emission schedule and avoid running out of Qus too early ([Blog Post]([https://qubic.org/blog-detail/qubic-s-revolutionary-tokenomics-and-halvings](https://qubic.org/blog-detail/qubic-s-epoch-175-halving-understanding-its-role-in-tokenomics))).

---

##  Considerations

Should the halving not happen, the max supply may be reached within the next 50-70 weeks.
Reaching max supply within 2027 puts Qubic and future ecosystem projects at risk and shortens the timeline for everyone.

Tokens locked in QEarn (TVL) are counted toward circulating supply. They can re-enter the market at any time.
Halving emissions helps to keep emissions going longer and gives the project time to grow.
