## Summary

This proposal asks Computors to approve a comprehensive governance and funding framework that establishes:
- Clear structural roles, mandates, and reporting lines for all ecosystem entities
- Strict separation between three funds (CCF, Incubation Fund, Listing Fund)
- Independent financial oversight through a Finance Auditing Entity
- Protocol primacy ensuring technical integrity overrides governance decisions

## Background

The Qubic ecosystem has grown to include multiple workgroups, boards, and funding mechanisms. As operations have scaled, the need for formalized governance structures has become evident. This framework addresses:

1. **Structural Clarity** ‚Äî Defining who does what, who reports to whom, and what authority each entity has
2. **Fund Separation** ‚Äî Ensuring funds are isolated by purpose with distinct controls
3. **Accountability** ‚Äî Establishing independent oversight and public transparency

## Framework Overview

### Fund Structure

| Fund | Purpose | Control |
|------|---------|---------|
| **CCF (Computor Controlled Fund)** | Funds Full Workgroups and approved Projects | Computor approval required |
| **Incubation Fund** | Dedicated to incubation projects only | Incubation Board |
| **Listing Fund** | Exchange listings only | Listing Board |

All three funds operate independently with strict isolation. Only CCF is subject to audit by the Finance Auditing Entity.

### Organizational Entities

**Full Workgroups** (funded by CCF):
- Marketing Workgroup
- Community Management Workgroup  
- Qubic Science Workgroup
- Tech Workgroup (with Protocol Primacy)

**Small Execution Workgroups** (funded by Full Workgroups):
- Business Development
- Incubation Management

**Strategy Layer Boards**:
- Strategic Board (WG Leads + Computor Delegates + Advisors) ‚Äî defines strategic direction, first arbiter for conflicts
- Incubation Board ‚Äî controls Incubation Fund, defines incubation program strategy
- Listing Board ‚Äî controls Listing Fund, decides exchange strategy

**Oversight**:
- Finance Auditing Entity ‚Äî independent oversight of CCF spending, mandatory access to financial records, public transparency

### Key Principles

1. **Fund Separation** ‚Äî Funds strictly separated by purpose with distinct controls
2. **Decision vs Execution** ‚Äî Boards decide direction; Workgroups execute
3. **Independent Oversight** ‚Äî Financial oversight is independent and mandatory
4. **Protocol Primacy** ‚Äî Protocol rules override all governance decisions; Tech Workgroup must refuse directives that violate protocol integrity

### Conflict Resolution

- **Standard conflicts**: Strategic Board ‚Üí Computors (final authority)
- **Protocol conflicts**: Tech Workgroup ‚Üí CFB ‚Üí Protocol (governance cannot override protocol constraints)
